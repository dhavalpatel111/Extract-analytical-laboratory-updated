About Us

Extract Analytical Laboratory is situated at Ahmedabad, India. which provides services in fields of Testing, Research & development. Extract Analytical Laboratory Provide Analytical testing Services to Pharmaceutical, Food, Cosmetics, Herbal, Beverage, Water, Soil, Spices, Dairy Products, Oils, Chemicals, Nutrition etc. as per National and International standards and specifics customer requirements. Equipped with most sophisticated Instrument like HPLC, GC, GC-HS,I CP-OES, AAS, FTIR, UV/VIS spectrophotometer etc. that facilitates latest trend of pharmaceutical analytical testing as well as advanced phytochemical research. We also Provide Service in the Area of Research and Hands-On Training for latest Analytical instrument. Approved BY ISO 9001:2015. Our highly educated and well experienced team , Analytical lab technician & trouble shooting experts along with advanced technologies and modern analytical equipment enhances our capability as an integrated service provider & enables us to deliver high speed quality testing services to our clients. Extract Analytical Laboratory is determined and focused about delivering accurate and precise results.
